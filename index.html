Roadmap

A transparent, phased approach focused on verifiable actions and long-term integrity.

Phase 1 – Foundation (Completed / Ongoing)

  • Token deployment on Solana
  • Fixed supply with mint & freeze authorities revoked
  • Initial liquidity added and locked
  • Public verification of on-chain actions
  • Core communication channels established

Phase 2 – Transparency & Community (Current)

  • Consistent, factual project updates
  • Organic community growth
  • On-chain charitable allocations tracked publicly
  • Listings on tracking platforms (Freshcoin, CoinVote, etc.)

Phase 3 – Utility Exploration

  • Development of non-financial, participation-based utility
  • Token-gated web experience (skill-based game / access features)
  • Continued transparency around development progress

Phase 4 – Sustainable Expansion

  • Gradual liquidity scaling aligned with organic demand
  • Broader visibility once fundamentals justify it
  • Ongoing support of the project mission through verifiable actions
